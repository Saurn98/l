INTRODUCTION

  • MULTITECH is a Guwahati, (Assam, India) based Company associated with Sales, service and project execution work related to Electrical Vehical Charager and Chargin station.
  • The founder of the Company, a Bachelor of Electrical Engineering, Started the diversified company in the year 2014 with experienced employees from different background.
  • MULTITECH is the first and only company in North East India to construck EV Charging stations since 2019 and the same has been constructed for Assam State Transport Corporation,A Government fo Assam organization.
  • Chargers of 120 KW have been installed for Charging of Buses.
  • Chargers of 30KW has been installed for four wheelers.
  • Six chargers in three Charging Stations have been installed till now and some more are under construction and in pipeline
  • We are authorised sales and service parthner of Charger of different types and capacity(BUS, FOUR WHEELER,THREE WHEELER, TWO WHEELER) for the entire North Eastern States.
